How Does It Work?

What do I want? Why do I want it? And, how will I achieve it?

1. Discover
My process begins with a comprehensive brief about your business and brand, mission, objectives and other relevant business information for technical implementation. In this step we do a workshop where we uncover relevant information, get on the same page about the company needs and requirements.
We define the right objectives for the project, we discover where the company is and where it wants to go.
2. Understand & Research
As part of my technical process, I look at the perceived brand image in the marketplace as well as consumer interactions. I analyse the market, market trends and consumer behaviour as well as their needs. Empathy is crucial in finding the correct insights that will later lead to relevant user experience. Unbiased research helps me understand what is stopping your brand from reaching its goals.
3. Test & Clarify
This is where we draw some conclusions and extract relevant insights. We talk them over and make sure they are in line with your company needs and desires. While it may seem like a trivial step in the technical process, it is the most important.
Clarifying is the key the starting point in building a good process which then leads to a valuable implementation for your operations. Defining the approach, tactics and actions and linking them to objectives and KPIs are also a part of this step.
My aim is to deliver the best results through the technical implementation. After we validate assumptions together, we start crafting a solution. And before any work is done, we craft a realistic timeline and budget.
4. Implement & Deploy
After we have everything in order, we deploy the best solution for your company. We take all plans and materials developed during the previous steps and we make them happen.
